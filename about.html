About Multispect

A house of expertise — cultural, regional, human.

We pair experienced analysts with modern AI, applying each to what it does best — without substituting one for the other.

§ VI · Who We Are

Not a platform. A house of expertise.

01

Cultural fluency

Our analysts work natively across Arabic and other Middle Eastern, Slavic and European languages, interpreting the cultural register rather than the literal text — the religious allusion, the in-group idiom, the historical reference automated translation cannot capture.

02

Domain depth

We serve diplomatic missions, municipal authorities and security-adjacent clients across some of the most sensitive theatres in the world — Israel, the wider Middle East, and Western Europe. Each engagement is tailor-made for the client and the context.

03

AI as instrument, not product

Modern AI supports every stage — narrative detection, sentiment modelling, source mapping, translation triage. The intelligence is delivered as a human-authored assessment with fully auditable sourcing. The client receives analytical judgment, not access to a generic tool.

04

Boutique by design

Our services are led by senior, seasoned analysts with direct responsibility for delivery, combining a bespoke approach with deep, first-hand expertise. The client works with the people who produce the intelligence.

§ IX · Ethics & Governance

Understanding, not surveillance.

Lawful scope, group-level reading and human authorship — designed in, not retrofitted. Both an ethical commitment and a clean break from the opaque security-vendor market.

01

Open-source only.

We work from public and open material. No intrusion, no covert collection — a basis that survives scrutiny.

02

Auditable sourcing.

Every assessment carries its chain of evidence — sourcing that holds up in court, in committee, or in the press.

03

Groups, never individuals.

We assess the temperature of places and publics — not the propensity of named persons. Aligned with the EU AI Act’s red lines (Art. 5).

04

Human-authored judgment.

AI collects and triages; every conclusion is written by a named analyst who stands behind it. Understanding, not automation.

From reaction to understanding — and, where needed, prevention.

Concept · The Spectrum

We read communities across the full spectrum.

And we tell you where on it tension — or change — is moving. Security is not the centre of the instrument. It is the far end of a scale that begins in the cultural and civic life of a place.

§ II · The Operating Problem

The fog of social friction.

Decision-makers routinely operate in a fog about the internal depth of distinct communities. Three forces keep them blind — a visibility gap (the codes aren’t legible), the speed of escalation (a rumour outruns the response), and generic-tool noise (volume without meaning).
